--[[ Slate help

-- How do I open files?
Drag and drop files from your desktop or from
file navigator

-- How do I close tabs?
Right click to open a menu, middle click to force

-- How do I edit my settings?
Edit the "prefs.pod" inside of the launcher

-- What does the "*" next to a tab mean?
It means you have unsaved changes in that file

-- What do the monitor and folder button do?
The monitor button opens a terminal, the folder
button opens a folder in the location of the open
file.

-- Where do I send feedback?
Comment on the bbs post
(https://www.lexaloffle.com/bbs/?tid=140703)

]]--
